- **Owner:** 🤖

- **Owner:** 🤖 (patch `package.json`)

### H-2 · Homepage forest image `src` hardcoded by `next.config.js`

- **Fix:** Verify image exists at `public/forest-*.jpg` or equivalent. If path is wrong, home page hero becomes broken.
